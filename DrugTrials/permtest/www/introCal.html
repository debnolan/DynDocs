Data Description

These data are from a drug trial testing whether calcium is effective in lowering blood pressure. 19 participants were randomly selected to receive one of two treatments. Before starting treatment, their blood pressure values were taken (mmHg). Then, 9 participants were given administered a set dose of calcium over a certain span of time, while the other 10 were given a placebo for the same time period. After the treatment was over, blood pressure values were again recorded. The table below shows the difference between the final and initial blood pressures for each participant.
