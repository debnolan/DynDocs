Autoregressive Model tutorial

Defination:
ln statistics and signal processing, an autoregressive (AR) model is a representation of a type of random process; as such, it describes certain time-varying processes in nature. It is a model frequently use in time series model. People use AR to estimate future trend from analysic information from the past. Formula:
Xt = c + ρ1Xt - 1 + ρ1Xt - 2 ... + ρpXt - p + εtt

Here, p is a indicator to see how many previous value is affecting the present value. εtt is the noise(error terms) for each value. Normally, it has a normal distribution of mean is 0 and standard derviation of σ. ρ1, ρ2,ρ3 ... ρp are the parameter that the previous values affecting the present value.
