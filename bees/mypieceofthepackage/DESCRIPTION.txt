Description: This package contains a tutorial which explores the consequences of varying the parameters of the Gamma Distribution. Additionally, the package covers a brief case study on the spread of the invasion of the Africanized Honey Bees to the United States from the data provided by Matix and Kiffe using the Gamma distribution.
